Inventory Components

slotGrid and player inventory.

Inventory components bridge UIWeaver's layout system with Minecraft's slot system.

slotGrid

ui.slotGrid(3, 3)
    .id("inputSlots")
    .bind("machineInv")

Renders a grid of item slots. Slot positions are computed from the layout pass and then registered as real Minecraft Slot objects at those positions.

Known limitation: Slot visual positions and actual Slot positions must stay perfectly synchronized. This is the most active area of improvement in UIWeaver. See Known Limitations for details.

Binding to an inventory

The bound key should resolve to an IItemHandler or Container registered in the ViewModel:

public final SyncedInv machineInv = syncInv("machineInv", handler);

Player inventory

To include the player inventory at the bottom of the screen:

ui.column()
    .add(machineArea)
    .add(ui.playerInventory())

playerInventory() automatically adds all 36 player slots at the correct positions.
